tags:
---



PA matrix $A$ is **positive definite** if for any non-zero column vector $x$, the product $x^{\top} A x$ is positive. In other words, $x^{\top} A x>0$ for all non-zero vectors $x$.

# Symmetric positive definite matrices

Examples:


$$
\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 0 \\
0 & 1
\end{bmatrix}
$$

$$
\begin{bmatrix}
2 & -1 \\
-1 & 2
\end{bmatrix}
$$

$$
D = \begin{bmatrix}
3 & 0 \\
0 & 4
\end{bmatrix}
$$
